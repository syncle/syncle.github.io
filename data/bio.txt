Jaesik Park is an Assistant Professor at POSTECH. He received his Bachelor’s degree from Hanyang University in 2009, and he received his Master’s degree and Ph.D. degree from KAIST in 2011 and 2015, respectively. Before joining POSTECH, He worked at Intel as a research scientist, where he co-created the Open3D library. His research interests include image synthesis, scene understanding, and 3D reconstruction. He serves as a program committee at prestigious computer vision conferences, such as Area Chair for ICCV, CVPR, and ECCV.

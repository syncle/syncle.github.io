Lidar scan

Lidar scan data was collected using a FARO Focus 3D X330 HDR scanner. The scanner has an operating range of 0.6m to 330m. At a distance of 10 meters, its ranging accuracy is 0.1 millimeters. Each scene was scanned from multiple locations. The scans were merged using dedicated software provided by the manufacturer, which is used for range scan alignment in industrial applications.

RGBD scan

In each scene, we captured a continuous RGB-D video sequence using an Asus Xtion Live camera. The lengths of the sequences range from 11 to 18 minutes. Each sequence thoroughly covers the respective scene. The RGB-D sequences can be used as input to scene reconstruction systems.

Jaesik Park is an Assistant Professor at Seoul National University. He received his Bachelor’s degree from Hanyang University, and he received his Master’s degree and Ph.D. degree from KAIST, advised by Dr. Yu-Wing Tai and Prof. In So Kweon. He was a research scientist at Intel intelligent systems lab. led by Dr. Vladlen Koltun, and he co-created the Open3D library. Before joining Seoul National University, he was a faculty member at POSTECH. His research interests include image synthesis, 3D perception, and computer vision topics. He serves as a program committee at prestigious computer vision conferences, such as ICCV, CVPR, and ECCV.
